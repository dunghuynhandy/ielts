# Cities

> Sources: NgocBach Q1 2022, NgocBach Q1 2024, NgocBach Q2 2022 (May–Sep), NgocBach Q3

## Key Ideas (IELTS Simon)

### Negatives
- Life in cities has its drawbacks.
- The cost of living is higher than in rural areas.
- Some people do not manage to find work.
- Housing is usually much more expensive.
- Homelessness and poverty are common in cities.
- There is a gap between the rich and poor.
- Life in cities can be extremely stressful.
- There are problems like traffic congestion and crime.
- Cities lack a sense of community.
- People do not even know their neighbours.
- Cities are sometimes described as “concrete jungles”.
- Pedestrian Areas
- Pedestrian zones in city centres can improve the local environment.
- Banning cars encourages people to walk or cycle.
- Many European cities have built bicycle lanes.
- Dependence on cars is linked to health problems like obesity.
- People who walk or cycle regularly are generally healthier.
- Pedestrian areas are safer and more attractive for both residents and tourists.

## Part 2 — Cue Cards (6 total)

### Cue card 1
_Source: NgocBach Q1 2022_

**Describe a place in the countryside that you visited**

You should say:
- Where it is
- When you visited this place
- What you did there
- And explain how you feel about this place

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Is there anything special about the countryside in Vietnam?
2. What do people usually do when going to the countryside?
3. Do you think people will live in the countryside in the future?

_Source: NgocBach Q1 2022_

**Describe a famous city that you think is interesting**

- Where it is
- What the city is famous for
- Why it is interesting
- And explain how you feel about it
- What it is
- When your family had it
- How your family got it
- And explain why it is important for your family

4. Do you think well-developed tourism will have negative effects in local people?
5. Something important to you that your family has had for a long time.?
6. What things do familes keep for a long time?
7. What’s the difference between things valued by people in the past and today?
8. What kinds of things are kept in museums?
9. What’s the influence of technology on museums?

### Cue card 3
_Source: NgocBach Q1 2024_

**Describe a park or a garden in your city**

You should say:
- When you often go there
- Where it is
- Who you often go there with
- And explain what it is like

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Do young people like to go to parks?
2. What do old people like to do in parks?
3. What are the benefits of going to the park for young people and old people?
4. Why do some people like planting flowers?

_Source: NgocBach Q1 2024_

**Describe a beautiful city that you have visited**
