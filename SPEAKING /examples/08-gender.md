### Part 1 (5 questions)

1. Do men and women have the same hobbies in your country?
2. Are there jobs that are mainly done by men or women?
3. Who does most of the housework in your home?
4. Do you think gender roles are changing?
5. Should men and women be paid equally for the same work?

### Part 2 — Cue Cards (2 cards)

#### Cue card 1

**Describe a person who has a job that is not typical for their gender**

You should say:
- who this person is
- what their job is
- what they do in their job
- and explain why their job is unusual for their gender

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Should men and women be treated equally in the workplace?
2. Are there differences in how boys and girls are educated?
3. How have men's and women's roles in the family changed?
4. Will gender equality improve in the future?
5. Why are some professions still dominated by one gender?

#### Cue card 2

**Describe a woman or man you admire**

You should say:
- who this person is
- what they have achieved
- what qualities they have
- and explain why you admire them

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Do you think women face more challenges in the workplace than men?
2. Should governments introduce quotas for women in leadership roles?
3. How can society promote gender equality?

### Part 3 — Discussion (4 questions)

1. Is it important for children to have both male and female role models?
2. How does the media portray men and women differently?
3. Should boys and girls play the same sports at school?
4. What can be done to reduce the gender pay gap?
