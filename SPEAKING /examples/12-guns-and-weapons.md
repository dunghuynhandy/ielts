#### Cue card 1

**Describe a film or game that involves weapons or fighting**

You should say:
- what it is
- what happens in it
- who you watched or played it with
- and explain how you felt about it

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Should ordinary citizens be allowed to own guns?
2. What role should the police play regarding weapons?
3. How can the international arms trade be controlled?

#### Cue card 2

**Describe a news story about violence or conflict you remember**

You should say:
- what the story was about
- where and when it happened
- how you heard about it
- and explain how it made you feel

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Why does violence appear so often in the news?
2. How can countries reduce armed conflict?
3. Should military spending be reduced?
