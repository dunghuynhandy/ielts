### Part 2 — Cue Cards (2 cards)

#### Cue card 1

**Describe a traditional product or custom in your country**

You should say:
- what it is
- how old it is
- who still uses or practises it
- and explain why it is important

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. Why are traditional skills disappearing?
2. Should governments protect traditional customs?
3. Is modern life better than life in the past?
4. How can young people learn about their cultural heritage?

#### Cue card 2

**Describe a traditional festival in your country**

You should say:
- what the festival is
- when it is celebrated
- what people do during the festival
- and explain why it is important

**Part 3 follow-up questions:**
1. How have festivals changed over time?
2. Do young people still care about traditional festivals?
3. Should traditional customs be taught in schools?

### Part 3 — Discussion (3 questions)

1. What traditional skills are worth preserving?
2. How does globalisation threaten local traditions?
3. Can tradition and modernity coexist?
