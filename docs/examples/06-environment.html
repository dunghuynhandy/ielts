Cue card 1

Source: NgocBach Q3

Describe a person who helps you to protect the environment

You should say: - Who this person is - How this person protects the environment - What difficulty this person has faced - And how you feel about this person

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. What kinds of pollution are serious in your country? 2. What can individuals do to protect our environment? 3. Do you think individuals should be responsible for pollution? 4. Why is there a need to involve government in environmental protection? 5. For VN people, how importance is protecting the environment? 6. In the future, what do you think will be some of the effects of global warming? 7. What is the importance of environmental education? 8. How to protect the environment?

Cue card 2

Source: NgocBach Q3

Describe an important plant in your country

You should say: - What the plant is - How you know it - Why it is important - And explain how much you like the plant

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. Do people in your country like to grow plants at home? 2. Do old people grow plants? 3. How do schools teach students to grow plants? 4. What is the main plant in your country?
