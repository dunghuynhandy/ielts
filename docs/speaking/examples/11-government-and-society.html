Part 2 — Cue Cards (2 cards)

Cue card 1

Describe a public facility (e.g. library, hospital, park) in your area

You should say: - what it is - where it is - what people use it for - and explain how important it is to the community

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. What services should governments provide for citizens? 2. Should governments censor the media? 3. Are security cameras in public places a good idea? 4. How can society better support people with disabilities? 5. Should voting be compulsory?

Cue card 2

Describe a time you did voluntary work or helped your community

You should say: - what you did - when and where it happened - who you helped - and explain how you felt about it

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. Why do some people volunteer? 2. Should governments fund voluntary organisations? 3. How can communities become more cohesive?

  2. Should governments provide free healthcare for all citizens?
  3. How can citizens hold governments accountable?
  4. Is censorship ever justified?
