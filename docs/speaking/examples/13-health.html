Cakes and other sweet things

Source: NgocBach Q1 2024

  1. Did you enjoy cakes and sweets when you were a child? (Why/ Why not?)
  2. Have you ever made a cake yourself? (Why/Why not?)
  3. On what occasions do people in your culture eat special cakes or other sweet food? (Why?)
  4. Do you enjoy eating something sweet at the end of a meal? (Why/Why not?)

Tea or Coffee

Source: NgocBach Q1 2024

  3. Doyou ever give visitors to your home tea or coffee? (Why/Why not?)
  4. Which is more popular in your country, tea or coffee? (Why?)

Part 2 — Cue Cards (2 total)

Cue card 1

Source: NgocBach Q3

Describe a time that you took a medicine

You should say: - When it happened - Who gave it to you - Why you had it - How you felt about it

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. Are medicines safe? Some people think all drugs are poisonous to some degree, do you? 2. Will you buy medicines on the internet? 3. Why do some people not like to take medicine? 4. Are there fake medicines? 5. What do you think of buying medicine online? 6. What do you think of fake medicine?

Cue card 2

Source: NgocBach Q3

Describe a time you were scared

You should say: - When it happened - Where it happened - Why you were scared - How you felt about it

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. How to scare others? 2. Are VN people easily scared? 3. Do kids like to scare others? 4. Why some people more likely to be scared? 5. What can we do when we are scared? 6. Is it OK to frighten others?
