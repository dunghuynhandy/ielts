Generated Practice Questions

Additional IELTS-style questions for practice (generated from topic themes).

Part 1 (5 questions)

  1. Are traditional customs still important in your country?
  2. Do you know any traditional skills?
  3. Do you prefer traditional or modern music?
  4. Has your country changed a lot in recent years?
  5. Do you celebrate traditional festivals?

Part 2 — Cue Cards (2 cards)

Cue card 1

Describe a traditional product or custom in your country

You should say: - what it is - how old it is - who still uses or practises it - and explain why it is important

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. Why are traditional skills disappearing? 2. Should governments protect traditional customs? 3. Is modern life better than life in the past? 4. How can young people learn about their cultural heritage?

Cue card 2

Describe a traditional festival in your country

You should say: - what the festival is - when it is celebrated - what people do during the festival - and explain why it is important

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. How have festivals changed over time? 2. Do young people still care about traditional festivals? 3. Should traditional customs be taught in schools?

Part 3 — Discussion (3 questions)

  1. What traditional skills are worth preserving?
  2. How does globalisation threaten local traditions?
  3. Can tradition and modernity coexist?
