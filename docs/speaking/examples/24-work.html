Cue card 5

Source: NgocBach Q1 2024

Describe a peson who is good at teamwork

You should say: - What team he/she joined - What work he/she did - What his/her role was - And explain what makes him/her a good member of that team - Rounding-off questions: - What do other people in the team think about him/her?

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. How do other people in your team think he is? 2. What do you think are the characteristics of a good leader? 3. What happens if you don’t have a good leader? 4. Why do some people not like teamwork? 5. Do you think young people are suitable for teamwork?

Part 2 — Cue Cards (2 cards)

Cue card 1

Describe a job you would like to do in the future

You should say: - what the job is - what skills it requires - why it interests you - and explain why you would be good at it

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. Is it better to stay in one company for life or change jobs regularly? 2. What are the advantages and risks of self-employment? 3. How should governments help unemployed people? 4. Has technology changed the way people work?

Cue card 2

Describe your ideal working environment

You should say: - what kind of workplace it would be - who you would work with - what facilities it would have - and explain why this environment appeals to you

Part 3 follow-up questions: 1. What makes a good employer? 2. Should companies offer flexible working hours? 3. How important is work-life balance?

Part 3 — Discussion (4 questions)

  1. Will robots replace many jobs in the future?
  2. Should the minimum wage be increased?
  3. Is job satisfaction more important than salary?
  4. How can workers adapt to a changing job market?
