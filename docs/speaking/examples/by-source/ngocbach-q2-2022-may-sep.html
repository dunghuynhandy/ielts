NgocBach Q2 2022 (May–Sep)

Full question list extracted from this PDF.

Part 2 & 3 (12 cue cards)

Cue card 1

Describe an important river/lake in your country

You should say: - Where it Is located - How big/long it is - What it looks like - And explain why it is important - How can rivers/lakes benefit local people? - How do rivers/lakes affect local tourism?

Cue card 2

Describe an Interesting neighbor

You should say: - Who he/she is - How you knew him/her - What you do together - And explain why he/she is interesting - How can people improve the relationship with neighbors in a community? - How do children build relationships with others in a community?

Cue card 3

Describe a time you were very busy

You should say: - When It happened - Where you were - What you did - And explain why you were busy - What are the advantages and disadvantages when people keep busy? - What kind of pressure people may experience at work?

Cue card 4

Describe a family member who you want to work with in the future

You should say: - Who he/she is - Whether you worked together before - What kind of work you would like to do with him/her - And explain how you feel about this family member - [ Part 3 j - What kinds of family business are common in Vietnam? - Why do people want to do family business? - What benefits are there when working for big companies?

Cue card 5

Describe a family member who you want to work with in the future

You should say: - Who he/she is - Whether you worked together before - What kind of work you would like to do with him/her - And explain how you feel about this family member - What kinds of family business are common in China? - Why do people want to do family business? - What benefits are there when working for big companies?

Cue card 6

Describe something you do that can help you con-centrate on work/study

You should say: - What it is - How it helps you concenưate - When you do it - And explain bow you feel about it - Why is it more difficult for children to concentrate nowadays than in the past? - What kinds of jobs require high concentration at work?

Cue card 7

Describe something that surprised you

You should say: - What it is - How you found out about it - What you did - And explain whether it made you happy - How do people express happiness in your cul¬ture? - How can people be happy?

Cue card 8

Describe an important event you celebrated

You should say: - What the event was - When it happened - Who attended the event - And explain how you feel about the event - What kinds of events do people usually cele¬brate?

Cue card 9

Describe a popular/well-known person in your country

You should say: - Who this person is - What he/she has done - Why he/she is popular - And explain how you feel about this person - What kinds of people are popular at work? - Which one is more important, keeping a good relationship with colleagues or doing well at work? - What benefits are there when a child is popular at school?

Cue card 10

Describe a positive change that you made in your life

You should say: - What it is - Why you did it - Who you did it with - And explain how you feel about the change - How can people change their daily routine if they want to? - What are the disadvantages when people keep making changes?

Cue card 11

Describe a time when you were stuck in a traffic jam.

You should say: - When and where it happened? - How long you were in the traffic jam? - What you did while waiting? - And explain how you felt when you were in that traffic jam.

Cue card 12

Describe a special day out (A day out which does not cost too much)

You should say: - Where did you go on that day. - What did you do. - Who did you go out with. - Why was it special to you.
